Inclined-Chest Press

  • Bulk
    1. 3 sets X 12 reps (increase the weight by 2.5kg after every set on both sides)
  • Cut
    1. 5 sets X 20 reps (constant and convenient weight for every set)
  • Weight Loss
    1. 6 sets X 25 reps (less weight and fast reps as you have to loose fat and make the fiber strong)
